About Me

I am a passionate bilingual educator with a Hispanic-Canadian background, dedicated to helping students master English and Spanish through innovative and engaging teaching methods.

Currently working as a Teacher and Conversation Assistant at Colegio Cisneros Alter, where I design and implement comprehensive lesson plans for students from Kindergarten to High School. My approach focuses on creating an inclusive, dynamic learning environment that addresses diverse educational needs.

With certifications including CELTA, DELE A1/A2 Examiner, and a Master's Degree in Bilingual Primary Education, I combine theoretical knowledge with practical experience to deliver exceptional educational outcomes.
